Sentiment Scraper

ACCESS CUSTOMER REVIEW DATA ONLINE AND USE NLP TO ANALYZE AND DISPLAY SENTIMENTS.

+

This framework focused on collecting, parsing, analyzing and tracking the emotions, sentimental value of customer reviews online, in order to predict, diagnose and improve customer expreience.

Data Collection

WEB SCRAPING.

PYTHON BEAUTIFULSOUP.

+

Data collection was based on various of datasets, acquired from main social media networks focused on travelling such as Reddit, Facebook, Kayak.

NPL

GOOGLE NATURAL LANGUAGE PROCESSING FRAMEWORK.

+

The Google Natural Language Processing Framework was heavily utilized in order to extract, parse and analyze data more precisely. Intensity ratings and trackings were assigned in order to figure whether a comment or review has positive or negative impact and the severity of the post.
